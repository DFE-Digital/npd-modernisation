SES (Socio economic status)

Contains information relating to income and free school meals. Updated frequently and available for use within the SRS.

Sub categories

FSM (Free school meals)

In England a Free School Meal (FSM) is a statutory benefit available to school-aged children from families who receive other qualifying benefits and who have been through the relevant registration process.

IDACI (Income deprivation affecting children indices)

The income deprivation affecting children index (IDACI) is an index of deprivation used in the United Kingdom. The index is calculated by the Office of the Deputy Prime Minister and measures in a local area the proportion of children under the age of 16 that live in low income households.
