Free school meals (FSM)

Information here about the types of data collected for FSM. Where is the data collected from and what can it be used for?

FSM

Captures if a pupil has ever been recorded as eligible for free school meals.

FSM past 6 years

Captures if a pupil has ever been recorded as eligible for free school meals in the last 6 years up to the pupil’s current year (not including nursery).

FSM past 3 years

Captures if a pupil has ever been recorded as eligible for free school meals in the last 3 years up to the pupil’s current year (not including nursery).

Related sub-categories

Income deprivation affecting children indices (IDACI)

IDACI is an index of deprivation used in the United Kingdom.
