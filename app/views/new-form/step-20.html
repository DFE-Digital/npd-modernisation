Your right to access the data

Sharing of pupil data via the The National Pupil Database relies on section 537A of the Education Act 1996. And the law which governs the sharing of NPD data with named bodies and third parties and to other bodies is the Education (Individual Pupil Information) (Prescribed persons) (England) Regulations 2009. These third parties and bodies are legally allowed access when “for the purpose of promoting the education or well-being of children in England are conducting research or analysis, producing statistics or providing information, advice or guidance”. This is cited in the relevant privacy notices for pupils and workforce in schools, for local authorities and for children in need and children looked after.
