Datasets

Census information

Alternative Provision Census (65)

Alternative Provision census data on children in Alternative Provision i.e. a school not maintained by an LA but which the authority is paying full tuition fees for.

Early Years Census (132)

Early years census data. This has information on children attending any private, voluntary and independent (PVI) sector nursery with one or more children receiving funding from the Department.

School Census (123)

School Census data for all pupils attending maintained schools. Collected from Spring 2013/14, this includes local authority (LA) maintained PRUs and alternative provision (AP) academies, including AP Free Schools.

Pupil Referral Unit Census (60)

Pupil Referral Unit census data. This has information on children attending local authority (LA) maintained PRUs and alternative provision (AP) academies, including AP Free Schools.

Absence and Exclusions

Absence (213)

This has information on pupil absences derived from the termly School Census.

Exclusions 2001-02 to 2004-05 (20)

Information on all recorded authorised and unauthorised absence and exclusion. Includes reasons, dates and the amount of time missed in sessions.

Exclusions 2005-06 onwards (91)

This has information on pupil exclusions as collected in the termly School Census (new format).

Childrens services

Children in Need (110)

This has information covering all children who are referred to children’s social care services.

Children Looked After (30)

This has information on children looked after by local authorities in England.

Independent Specialist Providers (86)

This has information on Independent Specialist Providers.

Educational stages

Early Years Foundation Stage Profile (180)

Early Years Foundation Stage Profile data of children in the final year of the Foundation Stage (Reception year).

Key Stage 1 (127)

Key stage 1 attainment data of learners by the end of year 2 of schooling.

Key Stage 2 (511)

Key stage 2 attainment data of learners by the end of year 6 of schooling.

Key Stage 3 (290)

Key stage 3 attainment data of learners by the end of year 9 of schooling.

Key Stage 4 (1311)

Key stage 4 attainment data (all methodologies) of learners by the end of year 11 of schooling.

Key Stage 5 (290)

Key stage 5 attainment data of post-16 learners in school sixth forms and FE colleges.

Post-16 Learning Aims (31)

This has information on post-16 learning aims as collected in the School Census.

Phonics (36)

Phonics Screening data of learners by the end year 1 of schooling.

Year 7 tests (68)

Year 7 progress tests data of learners by the end of year 7 of schooling.

Young Person’s Matched Administrative Dataset (304)

This has information on level of achievement at age 19

National Client Caseload Information (90)

This has information from the National Client Caseload Information System on employment destinations.

Childrens services

AP Pupil Addresses (16)

This has information on pupil addresses as collected in the Alternative Provision Census.

Pupil Addresses (15)

This has information on pupil addresses as collected in the School Census.

Categories

Absence and exclusion

Information on all recorded authorised and unauthorised absence and exclusion. Includes reasons, dates and the amount of time missed in sessions.

Attainment

Information on pupil performance including school and exam registration, foundation stage profiles, value-added calculations, prior attainment, predicted scores, exam results and pupil inclusion.

Demographics

Information about pupils and their school including personal and family data, where they live, the school they go to and when they started there.

Childrens services

Information on children in need (CIN) referred to children's services and children looked after (CLA) who’re in education and looked after by local authorities.
